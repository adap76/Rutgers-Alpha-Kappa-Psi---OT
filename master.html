About Omicron Tau

Our History & Legacy: Beyond the Classroom

Founded at Rutgers University, the Omicron Tau Chapter has built a powerful legacy as the premier business fraternity, dedicated to leadership and excellence. Our current chapter of 40+ active brothers and 300+ distinguished alumni forms a lifelong network of support and professional opportunity, extending far beyond the New Brunswick campus and into every major industry.

  • Lifelong Brotherhood and Networking: Cultivate deep, professional relationships that translate into a robust, global alumni network, providing continuous career guidance and opportunity.
  • Mentorship & Career Acceleration: Structured mentorship programs connecting brothers with alumni in top-tier firms (J.P. Morgan, Deloitte, Goldman Sachs, etc.), coupled with professional workshops focused on corporate skills, interview readiness, and advanced technical knowledge.
  • Community Service & Ethical Leadership: Commit to mutual service through impactful community initiatives like the Rutgers Dance Marathon, fostering ethical decision-making and a dedication to societal betterment.

Diversity & Inclusion

We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where brothers of all backgrounds, majors, and perspectives drive innovation. Our strength lies in the diversity of our members.
