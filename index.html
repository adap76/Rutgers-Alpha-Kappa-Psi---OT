About Omicron Tau

Our History & Legacy

Founded at Rutgers University, Omicron Tau Chapter has built a legacy of leadership, professional development, and community service. We currently have 40+ active brothers and over 100 alumni who continue to excel in business and beyond.

  • - Mentorship programs connecting brothers with alumni.
  • - Professional workshops and career preparation.
  • - Lifelong brotherhood and networking opportunities.
